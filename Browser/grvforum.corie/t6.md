Good catch, i modded two 4s and a 5, DO NOT RIP THE ICE NODE OUT. Everyone says: just cut it but that will fuck your inner composite shell, The board is anchored to a load-baring brace, if you remove it improperly the helmet will develop micro-flex issues in CF stress.  
  
  Instead leave the board seated, sever the antenna trace, install passive carbon shield between int, reroute to your own short-range unit.  
  
  That way you can just reroute it and still use the benefits

Also the type of helmet matters the weifght is not the same throughout. Mark 5 frontal plating is about 11-13% heavier than necessary for graving. People compensate by over tightening the chin strap but the whole fuckin helmet needs to be rebalances not clamped down.  
  
  I removed the secondary shock insert and replaced it wirth a lighter foam composite then added a counterweight strip in the rear ring. It shifted the center of mass back by under a CM but makes a huge difference, you can feel it
