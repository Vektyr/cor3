# Best places for a first exit (if you don’t want to die)

**Author** GripFail&emsp;**Posted** 4 years ago&emsp;**Status** None  

## Opening Post
I’ll say this right away so there’s no hysterics later: there are no ideal places. At all.  
  
If someone promises you a “calm first exit,” they’re either lying or they haven’t been out themselves in a long time.  
  
If you’re really pressed and decided to try anyway, I’d start with the old external maintenance rings around residential modules. Not because they’re “safe” (that word doesn’t exist here at all), but because things there are more or less familiar. A lot of old infrastructure, plenty of stuff built before they started stuffing smart sensors into every bolt.  
  
Don’t touch the new sectors. Seriously. Everything there is clean, pretty, and watching you far too closely. You won’t even understand where exactly you were spotted — at some point it just ends.  
  
And yes, I’ll say this upfront about the “southern mines.” If someone recommends them, it’s either a stupid joke, a test, or a person who wants to read the report about your disappearance later.  
  
I’m not saying the old rings are good. I’m saying that there you at least have a chance to understand what exactly went wrong.
