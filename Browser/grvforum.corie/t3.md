# For beginners: what is a grave and why do they hate you here

**Author** None&emsp;**Posted** None&emsp;**Status** None  

## Opening Post
Belt_archive: Read this before bouncing off cargo rails and calling it ‘graving’. A graver is not a sport or a game or a cult either, yet. Graving is not a trick, not a board, not a patch and sure as hell not an aesthetic.  
Graving is committing your full momentum to something actively trying to kill you, it’s crossing from control to surrender and pulling yourself back before the environment finishes the argument for you. The name came from gravity, but it stuck because of how often people don’t come back.  
  
These forum channels are here to extend the collaborative nature of this craft and share experience and tips, not to circle jerk each other. Why are some forums hostile to beginners? Because this is not a fashion forum, if we do not know you we do not know you. We aren’t trying to gatekeep this, everyone could do with learning the tools to break out of the system. We are friendly enough in person, so show up if you want smiles otherwise shut the fuck up, respectfully. If you want to learn, ask real questions. If you want validation, find a different community.
